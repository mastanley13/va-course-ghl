# Module 12 — Memberships & Communities (Optional/Advanced)

## Why it matters
Memberships/Communities are optional for the capstone, but they are common in client builds. They matter when the advertiser offer includes training, onboarding, or a partner portal.

## Learning outcomes
A passing VA can:
- Create a course structure (categories + lessons).
- Create offers for access (free/paid).
- Invite members and troubleshoot login access.
- Configure a community group and attach learning content.

## Vocabulary
- **Course**: structured content
- **Lesson**: unit inside a course
- **Offer**: access + pricing wrapper for a course
- **Member**: user with login access
- **Community group**: discussion + learning space

## Quiz (sample bank)
1) A course contains: **lessons/categories** ✅  
2) An offer controls: **pricing + access to course** ✅  
3) Communities include: **channels, posts, engagement** ✅  
4) Members access content via: **login portal** ✅  
5) Paid access is typically granted via: **purchase or workflow grant** ✅  
6) True/False: Deleting a course is always safe. **False** ✅  
7) A community group can: **include learning content** ✅  
8) Best practice for onboarding: **welcome email + clear next steps** ✅
