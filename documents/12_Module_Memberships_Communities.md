## Why it matters
Memberships/Communities are optional for the capstone, but they are common in client builds. They matter when the advertiser offer includes training, onboarding, or a partner portal.

## Learning outcomes
A passing VA can:
- Create a course structure (categories + lessons).
- Create offers for access (free/paid).
- Invite members and troubleshoot login access.
- Configure a community group and attach learning content.

## Vocabulary
- **Course**: structured content
- **Lesson**: unit inside a course
- **Offer**: access + pricing wrapper for a course
- **Member**: user with login access
- **Community group**: discussion + learning space

## Scenario walkthroughs with decision points
- **Onboarding new members**
  - Decision: auto-enroll or manual? ‚Üí Auto-enroll after payment; manual for scholarship/partner access.
  - Decision: welcome via email or SMS? ‚Üí Use email for links and policy, SMS only if consented for reminders.
- **Moderating community posts**
  - Decision: delete or coach? ‚Üí Hide harmful content, but coach members on formatting and tagging to keep engagement organized.
  - Decision: who can post announcements? ‚Üí Limit to moderators/admins to avoid noise.
